About Us

Labra Games Studio, founded by CEO Umar Sajid and Co-founder Aasia Khalid in June 2024, is a dynamic team of passionate game developers committed to delivering captivating and addictive hypercasual mobile games. Our mission is to create immersive experiences that keep players engaged and entertained for hours on end. With a focus on thrilling puzzles and engaging simulations, we are dedicated to crafting games that resonate with a wide audience.
